## 🌍 What is SKYRA?

SKYRA is a **Commodity Intelligence Platform** and **Verified B2B Network** designed to facilitate safe, transparent, and efficient international commodity trading and trade finance.

✅ Connect verified commodity traders worldwide
✅ Provide real-time market intelligence
